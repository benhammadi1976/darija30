# D81 Level 4 Web Research Notes — Souk & Shopping Better

## Sources consulted
- Daily Darija shopping/souk phrases: confirms practical phrases for asking prices, buying food, and interacting with sellers.
- Speak Moroccan bargaining forum: confirms bargaining expressions such as expensive, lower the price, budget limits, good price, and coming back.
- Travel/culture guides on Moroccan souks: confirm bargaining is expected in many souks, but should stay friendly and polite; fixed-price shops also exist.
- Souk/bargaining travel guides: confirm walking away politely, comparing prices, product quality, and packaging are important real tourist behaviors.

## D81 content decision
Level 4 is not a word list. It is a 30-day shopping/souk simulation track:
1. price questions
2. polite bargaining
3. colors/sizes/quality
4. food/fruit/spices/gifts
5. leather/textiles/ceramics
6. fixed-price vs bargaining
7. payment/change/receipts
8. exchanges/problems/packing
9. pressure-free exits
10. final mixed souk simulation

## Production rule
All sentences remain drafts until native Moroccan Darija QA. No audio, image, or video should be produced before that review.
