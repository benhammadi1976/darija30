# D76 — Level 2 Web-Researched Situation Sentences

## Scope

This phase writes the first full sentence pack after Level 1: **Level 2 — Guest / Riad / Hospitality**.

It does **not** produce or upload audio, images, or videos. It creates a researched draft sentence layer that must pass native Moroccan Darija QA before media production.

## Inputs

- Base project: `darija30_clean_d75_12_level_situation_matrix.zip`
- Frozen rules: D74-B level/language independence and D75 scene matrix rules
- Level selected: Level 2 — Guest / Riad / Hospitality

## Web-research basis

The research pass focused on riad/hotel check-in, Wi‑Fi, breakfast, check-out, room services, guest etiquette, tea culture, and Moroccan politeness formulas.

- Daily Darija — Booking a Hotel in Morocco: https://www.dailydarija.com/blog/hotel-booking-darija-phrases-tips — Hotel/riad services: Wi‑Fi, breakfast, parking, check‑out, room questions.
- Bewildered in Morocco — Checking Into a Hotel or Riad: https://www.bewilderedinmorocco.com/hotel-darija/ — Riad/hotel check-in, breakfast, Wi‑Fi password, laundry, late check-out.
- Gateway2Morocco — Accommodation & Riad Phrases: https://www.gateway2morocco.com/learn-moroccan-arabic — Accommodation phrases: room, nights, breakfast included, see the room.
- Daily Darija — Moroccan Hospitality and Guest Etiquette in Darija: https://www.dailydarija.com/blog/moroccan-hospitality-and-guest-etiquette-in-darija — Thanking hosts, goodbye expressions, hospitality etiquette.
- Darija School — Polite Expressions in Moroccan Arabic: https://darijaschool.com/en/polite-expressions-in-moroccan-arabic-darija/ — Polite openings, excuse me, welcome, thank-you formulas.
- Roaming Camels Morocco — Moroccan Tea Culture: https://roamingcamelsmorocco.com/moroccan-tea-culture/ — Tea as hospitality, respect, guest order, ritual context.
- Moroccan Visa — Cultural Etiquette for Travelers to Morocco: https://moroccan-visa.com/cultural-etiquette-for-travelers-to-morocco/ — Food etiquette: taste what is offered, refuse gently, use respectful language.
- City Tours Morocco — First Time in Morocco guide: https://citytoursmorocco.com/first-time — Wi‑Fi availability, WhatsApp/local communication realities, medina navigation.

## Core decision

D76 follows the Darija30 rule:

```text
Level → Day → Situation → Sentence
then later:
Sentence → Moroccan audio → mental image → video
```

The media is not the source. The sentence/situation is the source.

## What makes Level 2 different from competitors

Many Darija resources cover hotel vocabulary, greetings, and tea phrases. D76 turns those into lived micro-scenes: a learner arrives at a riad, confirms a reservation, asks for Wi‑Fi, handles a room problem, accepts tea, refuses food politely, asks for privacy, and leaves with thanks.

Each row includes:

- situation context
- Friendly Latin Darija sentence
- optional Arabic-script support
- English meaning
- likely Moroccan reply
- cultural note
- segmented pronunciation parts
- image prompt seed
- video action seed
- source keys
