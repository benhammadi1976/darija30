# D29 Now You Speak Recorder Model

## Educational sequence
The learner moves through this flow:

1. Understand the situation.
2. Watch/hear the real-life video.
3. See the phrase and spelling.
4. Record themselves saying the phrase.
5. Play their voice and compare with the native audio.
6. Try again.

## Privacy model
- The recording uses the browser microphone.
- The audio is not uploaded.
- The audio is not stored in the backend.
- The recording is temporary for the current page session.

## Product reason
This transforms Darija30 from a passive phrase list into a speaking practice product.
The learner feels: “Now I am the traveler inside this real scene.”
