# Live Darija Check Roadmap

## Concept

After a learner finishes the 30 lessons, Darija30 can offer a premium live speaking test with a Moroccan teacher.

Possible names:

- Live Darija Check
- Final Moroccan Speaking Test
- Morocco Confidence Call

## Format

Duration: 15–30 minutes.
Mode: audio or video call.
Teacher: Moroccan speaker trained to test tourist scenarios.

## Test moments

The teacher tests whether the learner can handle:

1. Greeting and politeness.
2. Taxi destination and stop.
3. Café order.
4. Market price and polite bargaining.
5. Hotel or help situation.
6. “I don’t understand / repeat slowly.”

## Output for the learner

After the session, provide:

- pronunciation feedback,
- confidence score,
- cultural correction,
- 3 phrases to improve,
- optional completion badge/certificate.

## Monetization

Do not include this in MVP.
Use it later as an add-on:

- Starter Pack: $29 one-time.
- Live Darija Check: $19–$29 add-on.
- Bundle later: 30 lessons + live test.

## Product timing

This feature belongs after:

1. Free lesson validation.
2. Real audio quality validation.
3. Starter Pack purchase path.
4. At least a small group of paying users.
