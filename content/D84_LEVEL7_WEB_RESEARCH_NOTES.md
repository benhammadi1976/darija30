# D84 Level 7 Web Research Notes — Longer Stay / Daily Services

Scope: Level 7 turns the learner from short visitor into longer-stay resident/guest who can handle practical daily services in Morocco.

Web-research anchors used before drafting:
- Renting and longer stays require practical housing questions: neighborhood, furnishing, rent, deposit, contract, repairs, and tenant/landlord responsibilities.
- Daily services include Wi‑Fi/router, SIM/data recharge, water/electricity bills, laundry/cleaning, trash/building rules, repairs, delivery, nearby pharmacy/printing/supermarket, appointments, rent receipts, ATM/card/cash, and remote-work café needs.
- Competitor coverage often stops at basic travel phrases, so Darija30 Level 7 is built as living scenarios: what happened, what the learner says, what a Moroccan local/service person may reply, and what action happens next.

Important QA rule: all Level 7 sentences are draft web-researched lines and must be reviewed by a native Moroccan Darija speaker before any audio recording or media production.
