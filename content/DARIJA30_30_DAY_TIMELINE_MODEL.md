# Darija30 30-Day Timeline Model

## Decision

Darija30 should be presented as a fast 30-day plan, not as a slow multi-week course.

## Product rule

- 30 lessons.
- 1 lesson per day.
- The timeline starts from the learner subscription date.
- After purchase, all lessons should be available immediately.
- The day-by-day dates are guidance, not a hard lock.
- Before payment, lessons remain locked previews for the learner.
- Admin can still review locked lessons through admin preview.

## Page change

The `/app/lessons` page now shows:

- Demo subscription start date.
- Target finish date.
- Day ranges instead of Week labels.
- Each lesson card includes the exact calendar date from the selected start date.
