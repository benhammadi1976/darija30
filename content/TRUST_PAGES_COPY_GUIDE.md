# Darija30 Phase D13 — Trust Pages Copy Guide

## Goal

Add trust without making the product heavy.

Darija30's core promise remains:

```text
البساطة + الصوت + السيناريو + الفهم الثقافي
```

Trust pages should support this promise. They should not turn the product into a legal maze.

## Pages added

- `#/privacy`
- `#/terms`
- `#/refund`
- `#/support`

## Writing rules

1. Use plain English.
2. Keep the tone calm and beginner-friendly.
3. Be honest that Stripe, real accounts, analytics, and support email are not active yet.
4. Do not claim legal finality before review.
5. Keep the first public learning experience free and frictionless.

## Launch rule

Before enabling paid traffic or Stripe, replace placeholders with final production-ready copy:

- Real support email
- Refund window
- Payment provider wording
- Account/data storage wording
- Legal review if needed

## Product positioning

Trust pages should reinforce this idea:

```text
No surprises. No hidden subscription. No complicated language.
```
