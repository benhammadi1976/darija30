# Darija30 Blog Sample Policy — Phase D20

## Purpose

The blog is an SEO and trust layer, not the full course.

Each article should:
- answer a real search question,
- give a small useful taste,
- show why Darija30 is practical,
- move the reader to a free lesson.

## Article phrase model

Each blog article uses exactly 3 phrase/sample cards:

1. **Written Darija sample**
   - Friendly Latin Darija
   - English meaning
   - no audio

2. **English outcome teaser**
   - English phrase only
   - tells the reader what they will learn to say in Moroccan Darija inside the lesson
   - no Darija answer and no audio

3. **Audio sample**
   - Friendly Latin Darija
   - English meaning
   - one speaker button only

The order can change from article to article so the blog does not feel repetitive.

## Why

Darija30 already gives 3 full free lessons. The blog should not replace the lessons. It should create curiosity and show a sample, while protecting the value of the full lesson method:

- normal audio,
- slow audio,
- video dialogue,
- real situation,
- cultural intent,
- quick practice.

## Rule

Blog = 3 samples only.
Free lessons = full method.
Locked previews = English outcomes only.
Paid lessons = full Darija method.
