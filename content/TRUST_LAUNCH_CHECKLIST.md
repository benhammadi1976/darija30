# Darija30 Trust Launch Checklist

Use this before connecting Stripe or launching paid ads.

## Privacy

- [ ] Confirm whether accounts are active.
- [ ] Confirm whether progress is stored locally or in backend.
- [ ] Confirm whether analytics/cookies are used.
- [ ] Confirm whether email addresses are collected.
- [ ] Replace MVP placeholder text with final privacy copy.

## Terms

- [ ] Confirm paid product name: Starter Pack.
- [ ] Confirm price: $29 one-time.
- [ ] Confirm content access rules.
- [ ] Confirm no fluency guarantee wording.
- [ ] Confirm usage/copyright wording for lessons/audio.

## Refund

- [ ] Choose exact refund window.
- [ ] Choose exact support email.
- [ ] Define expected response time.
- [ ] Define payment provider refund flow.
- [ ] Make refund text visible before checkout.

## Support

- [ ] Buy or configure domain email.
- [ ] Verify support inbox.
- [ ] Create canned replies for audio, access, refund, login, and payment questions.
- [ ] Decide response SLA.
- [ ] Test support link from footer and pricing page.

## QA routes

- [ ] `#/privacy`
- [ ] `#/terms`
- [ ] `#/refund`
- [ ] `#/support`
- [ ] `#/pricing`
- [ ] `#/launch-qa`

## Final rule

Do not enable Stripe until the four trust pages are accurate enough for real public users.
