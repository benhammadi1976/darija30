## Content philosophy
Level 9 is for practical work and business communication in Morocco. It teaches the learner how to introduce themselves professionally, schedule meetings, use WhatsApp/email, speak at reception, discover client needs, explain a service, ask for quotes/invoices/payment proofs, handle deadlines and delays, negotiate politely, identify decision-makers, discuss documents/contracts, work from coworking spaces, coordinate with freelancers, handle complaints, network, ask trade-fair questions, and manage admin paperwork.

Each phrase includes:
- Darija Latin
- Arabic helper
- English meaning
- scenario
- possible Moroccan reply
- culture note
- pronunciation chunks
- image prompt seed
- video action seed
- future media paths
