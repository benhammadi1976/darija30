# Darija30 Locked Preview Policy

Locked lesson previews should not display the Moroccan Darija solution or provide phrase audio for all locked phrases.

## Correct preview model
Show English outcomes only:

- My name is John
- I am from America
- I am a tourist
- I am from...
- Nice to meet you

Then explain:

> Inside the full lesson, you will learn how to say these in Moroccan Darija with normal audio, slow audio, video dialogue, real situation, cultural intent, and quick practice.

## Why
The first 3 free lessons already demonstrate the full Darija30 method. Locked previews should create desire and clarity, not replace the full paid lesson.

## Free lesson model
Free lessons may show:

- Friendly Latin Darija
- Normal audio
- Slow audio
- Video dialogue
- Optional Arabic script
- Meaning
- Cultural intent
- Memory hook
- Quick check

## Locked preview model
Locked lessons should show:

- English outcomes
- Method promise
- Starter Pack CTA
- Option to return to free lessons
