# D76 Web Research Notes — Level 2 Guest / Riad / Hospitality

## Research intent

Before writing Level 2 sentences, the web research focused on real Moroccan accommodation and hospitality contexts rather than word lists.

## Main findings

1. Hotel/riad phrase guides consistently include Wi‑Fi, breakfast included, parking, room readiness, check-out, laundry, and room questions.
2. Moroccan hospitality is strongly tied to tea, warm greetings, and thanking the host before leaving.
3. Food/tea refusal must be polite: compliment first, give a reason, or accept a little.
4. Darija30 should not only teach words; every phrase must be a scene that can later become audio, image, and video.
