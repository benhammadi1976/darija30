# D31 Admin Audio Collapsible Day Model

## Admin principle
The learner product is organized as:

- 30 days
- 5 phrases per day
- 150 phrases total

The admin audio page should follow the same structure.

## New admin audio flow
Instead of showing 150 rows at once, the admin sees:

- Day 1 — 5 phrases
- Day 2 — 5 phrases
- Day 3 — 5 phrases
- ...
- Day 30 — 5 phrases

Each day can be opened or closed.

## Why this matters
Audio recording and file delivery should happen day by day.
This makes it easier to track what is missing, what is ready, and what needs recording.

## Day summary
Each day header shows:

- Normal audio progress
- Slow audio progress
- Video progress
- Visual progress
- Ready / Needs recording

## Storage
The open/closed state is saved locally in the browser.
No backend is used in this phase.
