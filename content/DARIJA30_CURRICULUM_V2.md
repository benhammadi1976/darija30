# Darija30 Curriculum V2

## Core philosophy

Darija30 teaches Moroccan Darija through real situations, not alphabet-first study.

Official learning rule:

```text
One lesson = 5 phrase pages.
One phrase page = one usable sentence in one Moroccan situation.
30 lessons × 5 pages = 150 practical phrase pages.
```

Each phrase page should contain:

1. Scenario — where the learner is.
2. Goal — what the learner wants to do.
3. Watch Scene — optional 5–12 second micro video.
4. Listen Normal — real Moroccan voice.
5. Listen Slow — real Moroccan voice recorded slowly, not artificially stretched.
6. Say This — Friendly Latin Darija.
7. Optional Arabic — support only, not the first wall.
8. Meaning — simple English meaning.
9. Cultural Intent — what Moroccans understand socially.
10. Memory Hook — one small reusable pattern.
11. Quick Check — immediate practice.

## Recording priority

Do not record all 150 phrases randomly.
Record in this order:

1. Free 15 phrases: Taxi, Cafe, Market.
2. The highest travel-risk phrases: taxi meter, stop here, help, pharmacy, hotel problems.
3. Blog phrase snippets used by SEO pages.
4. Remaining Starter Pack phrases.

## Product rule

Blog articles may teach phrases, but every phrase should be hearable when possible. A blog reader should become a listener, not only a reader.
