# AI Voice Cloning Test Plan

## Decision

AI voice can be tested, but it should not replace human Moroccan review.

Darija30’s quality comes from sounding natural in Morocco. Any AI-generated voice must be checked by a Moroccan speaker before being placed in paid lessons.

## Safe usage rule

Use only:

- the owner’s own voice, or
- a Moroccan speaker who gives clear written and voice consent.

Do not clone celebrities, strangers, or teachers without explicit permission.

## Test set

Start with only five phrases:

1. salam
2. bghit nmshi l-otel
3. qahwa kahla aafak
4. bsh-hal hada?
5. ghali bzaf

## QA checklist

For each generated phrase, check:

- Does it sound Moroccan, not foreign?
- Is the rhythm natural?
- Are hard sounds acceptable?
- Is the tone friendly and clear?
- Would a Moroccan understand it without laughing or correcting it?

## Recommended product use

- MVP: real human voice for free lessons.
- After MVP: AI-assisted voice may help create draft audio.
- Final paid content: every AI-assisted file must pass human QA.

## Site disclosure idea

If AI-assisted audio is used later:

```text
Some practice audio may be AI-assisted from a consented Moroccan voice and reviewed for learning quality.
```
