# Darija30 SEO Blog Strategy — Phase D10

## Product philosophy

Darija30 is not a generic blog about Morocco. It is an acquisition layer for an audio-first Darija product.

Every article should lead to a useful scenario lesson.

```text
Search intent → simple answer → useful Darija phrase → cultural intent → free audio lesson CTA
```

## Core audience

English-speaking learners who are about to travel to Morocco, meet Moroccan family, date/marry into Moroccan culture, or do business/social visits.

## Main SEO clusters

### 1. Travel phrases

- Moroccan Darija phrases for travel
- Moroccan Arabic travel phrases
- what to say in Morocco
- Moroccan taxi phrases
- Moroccan café phrases
- Moroccan market phrases

### 2. Confusion removal

- MSA vs Darija
- should I learn Arabic or Darija for Morocco
- Moroccan Arabic vs Arabic
- Moroccan Darija transliteration
- why Darija uses numbers

### 3. Scenario guides

- how to bargain in Morocco
- how to order coffee in Morocco
- how to take a taxi in Morocco
- how to greet people in Morocco

## Article rules

- Keep the article practical, not academic.
- Use Friendly Latin Darija first.
- Arabic script is optional and secondary.
- Avoid long grammar explanations.
- Include phrase rows with meaning and cultural notes.
- End every article with a CTA to the relevant lesson.

## Conversion rule

The blog is successful only if it moves the reader into the free lesson quickly.

Ideal path:

```text
Google search → Blog article → phrase examples → Try Free Audio Lesson → Pricing after 3 lessons
```
