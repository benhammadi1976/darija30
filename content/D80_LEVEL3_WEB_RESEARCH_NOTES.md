# D80 — Level 3 Web Research Notes

Level 3 focuses on Café & Daily Social Life, not as a vocabulary list but as real scenes: entering a café, ordering, waiting, paying, small talk, weather, social exits, and explaining that the learner is practicing Darija.

Research basis used:
- Moroccan café/restaurant phrase coverage: ordering tea/coffee/nass nass, asking for Wi‑Fi, menu, and bill.
- Moroccan social/café culture: cafés as daily social spaces, tea/coffee as social rituals, light conversation and hospitality.
- Darija small talk: greetings, how-are-you replies, weather comments, and learner-protection phrases such as asking someone to repeat or speak slowly.

Decision:
Level 3 should not copy competitor phrase lists. It becomes a 30-day café/social simulation: learner enters a café, orders, waits, chats, handles misunderstanding, explains they are learning Darija, and completes a two-minute social interaction.
