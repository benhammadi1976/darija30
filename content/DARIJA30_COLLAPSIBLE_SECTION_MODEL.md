# Darija30 Collapsible Section Model

The 30-day course is grouped into six collapsible sections:

1. Days 1–5 — Fast Survival Start
2. Days 6–10 — Core Confidence + Taxi
3. Days 11–15 — Transport, Café & Restaurant
4. Days 16–20 — Food Needs & Shopping
5. Days 21–25 — Hotel & Practical Help
6. Days 26–30 — Culture & Final Survival

Each section contains:
- 5 lessons
- 25 phrase pages
- a date range calculated from the learner start date

The accordion improves mobile readability and keeps the 30-day plan fast and clear.
