# Darija30 Competitive Positioning

## Core positioning
Darija30 is not a textbook, not a grammar course, and not a generic phrasebook.
It is a Moroccan situation trainer for English-speaking travelers.

## Why Darija30 is different
Most resources teach Darija like a language course. Darija30 teaches it like a real Moroccan situation.

The learner does not start with the alphabet. The learner starts inside a taxi, café, market, riad, or street moment.
They hear the phrase, repeat it, understand the cultural intent, and use it.

## Product structure to preserve
- 30 lessons
- 5 phrase pages per lesson
- 150 practical phrase pages total
- Normal + slow audio
- Friendly Latin first
- Optional Arabic script
- Micro video for key situations
- Cultural Intent for every phrase
- Quick Check practice
- Live Darija Check later as a premium add-on

## Design rule
Keep every page simple. Darija30 wins by being useful, fast, audio-first, and culturally clear.
