# D85 Level 8 Web Research Notes — Health, Safety & Problems

Scope: Level 8 teaches the learner to handle health issues, safety concerns, and practical problems in Morocco without turning the course into medical or legal advice.

Web-research anchors used before drafting:
- Darija health phrase resources emphasize symptoms, doctor/pharmacy communication, allergies, medicine instructions, and urgent help.
- Travel safety sources emphasize knowing emergency services and being ready to contact local help, police, medical services, and insurance/embassy support.
- Darija vocabulary resources consistently include tbib/doctor, sbitar/hospital, frmasiya/pharmacy, dwa/medicine, wja3/pain, and phrases for lost passport, stolen wallet, and calling police.

D85 writing rules:
- Keep each sentence situation-first and practical.
- Avoid giving medical/legal advice; the phrase helps the learner ask for professional/local help.
- Keep the learner language calm, direct, and respectful.
- All sentences are draft and must pass native Moroccan Darija QA before recording audio or creating media.
